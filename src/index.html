The Great Seattle Uniform Bracket

By Rich Boudet, Frank Mina and Evan Webeck | The Seattle Times

Seattle has seen its share of uniform duds and sensations over the years. One thing remains true: We’ve never been boring.

The Mariners incorporated their nautical name into their uniforms from the start and were the forbearers of the legendary Turn Ahead The Clock promotion. The SuperSonics started out electric, then went Space Jam with their look in the mid-90s. The Seahawks stayed static for 25 years, but now their ‘Action Green’ is the brightest and boldest color in the NFL.

Ultimately, it’s the fabric and everything represented within it — city pride, kinship, connection — for which fans root. Players come and go, but uniforms are forever.
