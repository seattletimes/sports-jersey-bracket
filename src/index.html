Brewery Battle

By Amy Wong, Stefanie Loh, Emily M. Eng and Jennifer Luxton | The Seattle Times

Seattle loves beer and is known for its ever increasing number of breweries. With Oktoberfest around the corner, we wanted to find out what Seattle’s favorite brewery is.

We received almost 2,000 submissions championing 150 breweries during the nomination process. We narrowed it down to 32, based on the number of submissions and with the help of our food and drink writer Tan Vinh. We’ve put together miniprofiles of each brewery along with some fun facts. Now happy voting and may your favorite brewery prevail.
